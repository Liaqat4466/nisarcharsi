Recipe of a Chicken Karhai

Chicken Karhai

The recipe and instructions I am sharing with you is for the spicy chicken karhai.

    Ingriedients for the recipe

  • Normal Chicken meat
  • Fresh Ginger
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Fresh green Chilli and Corainder
  • Spices of different types

    Method

  1. We start by cooking the chicken in a generous amount of Oil.
  2. We then add in an LOT of minced Ginger and Garlic.
  3. We then go ahead and add all our tomatoes and spices.
  4. We allow this mix to cook on High , stirring as needed to make sure it does not Catch at the bottom of Pan.
  5. Afer 30 minutes , the Karhai will be ready.
